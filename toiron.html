Josh's Tutorials - Speedrunning Minecraft

Part 1 - Getting Started

The first thing you need to do is find a tree. If you spawn in a desert, good luck finding one.

Once you do, punch it to get your first log. You use this log to make planks, and then use these to make your first crafting table!

Use the crafting table to make a wooden pickaxe. Find exposed stone on the mountains, or find a cave. Use the wooden pickaxe, to mine out your first stone!

Craft a stone pickaxe. Mine as much stone as possible to make furnaces. Kill at least three sheep to make a bed. Sleep to survive your first night.

Head to the caves and get deeper. Find some coal and iron ores. Mine them as much as possible until your stone pickaxe breaks. Go back up, head back to your bed. While smelting iron, use your blocks to build your first base!

Collect your iron, and make your first iron tools and armor!
